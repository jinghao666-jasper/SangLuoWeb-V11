Company Profile

Yongji Sangluowang Distillery Co., Ltd.

Established on the banks of the Yellow River in Yongji, Shanxi Province, Sangluowang Distillery is the modern inheritor of a spirit tradition dating back over 1,500 years to the Northern & Southern Dynasties.

Our master distillers preserve every step of the original earthenware-pit fermentation and copper-pot distillation — because authentic Sang Luo Jiu can only be made one way.

Learn more ›

Our Vision & Values

We believe great spirits are made with patience, respect for nature, and an unshakeable commitment to craft. Our vision is to bring the story of Sang Luo Jiu to the world — honouring 1,500 years of heritage while embracing the future.

Guided by the principle of manaaki — to care for and honour — we treat every partner, customer and visitor with the same respect we give our craft.
