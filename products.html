Sang Luo Jiu Signature

QINGXIANG BAIJIU · 蒲饭桑落

Our most celebrated expression — compact, elegant and unmistakably Sang Luo. Crafted from premium Yellow River wetland sorghum and fermented using our traditional earthenware-pit method. The matte-black bottle with hand-applied 24-carat gold calligraphy makes it as beautiful to display as it is to drink.

Clean and floral on the nose, silky on the palate with notes of ripe pear, white blossoms and a subtle mineral finish.
